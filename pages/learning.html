FAQs

Can I publish my findings online?

Yes, if you cite sources and respect copyright and cultural protocols.

What if sources disagree?

Note differing viewpoints and explain your method for comparing them.

How do I avoid plagiarism?

Quote carefully, paraphrase with attribution, and keep citation records.
